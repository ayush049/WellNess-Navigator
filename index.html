Therapies

"Therapy: Where Hope Meets Healing

Yoga Therapy

Yoga therapy blends yoga's physical postures, breathwork, and mindfulness to enhance overall well-being, alleviate specific health issues, and foster healing.

Sensory Integration Therapy

Sensory Integration Therapy helps individuals with sensory processing difficulties to improve sensory responses, focus, and daily functioning through structured sensory experiences.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Psychodynamic therapy explores unconscious processes and past experiences to understand and address present emotional and psychological issues, fostering insight and healing.

Interpersonal Therapy

Interpersonal Therapy (IPT) focuses on improving relationships and communication to alleviate depression and address relational issues, promoting emotional well-being.

Family Therapy

Family therapy involves the entire family in treatment, addressing relational dynamics and communication to foster healthier relationships and resolve conflicts.

Neurological Physical Therapy

Neurological physical therapy is specialized care that aids individuals with neurological conditions, such as stroke or multiple sclerosis, to improve mobility, balance, and independence.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is an ancient healing technique that involves inserting thin needles at specific points on the body to alleviate pain and promote overall wellness through balancing energy pathways.

Cardiac Rehabilitation:

Cardiac rehabilitation is a structured program for individuals recovering from heart-related conditions, focusing on exercise, education, and lifestyle changes to improve cardiovascular health.
