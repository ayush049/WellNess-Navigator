Depression Test:

A depression test is a tool to assess potential symptoms of depression. It includes questionnaires and interviews, but it's not diagnostic. Results should guide individuals to seek professional help. False positives and negatives are possible, and individual factors can affect responses. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a comprehensive evaluation

Clinical assessment is the cornerstone of mental health care. It's where empathy meets expertise, illuminating the path to healing and resilience. Through assessment, we navigate the complexities of the human mind with compassion and precision.
